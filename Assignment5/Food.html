One Pan Burrito Bowl

1 1 1/1.5 2 tbsp 1/2 pack 3/4 cup 1/2 can 1 1/2 1 stalk to taste to taste to taste to taste -Pepper -Onion -Limes -Oil -Taco Seasoning -Rice -Black Beans -Avocado -Tomato -Green Onion -Thawed Corn -Salsa -Sour Cream -Cheese Chop peppers and onions into strips, lightly sauté for 2min in 1tbsp oil, add juice of half a lime sauté for another minute. Remove to a bowl.

In the same pan add 1tbsp oil, taco seasoning and rice sauté for 1 min, add water and cook rice according to packet directions. When rice is almost cooked add black beans and corn. When cooked add peppers and onions, cheese, diced avocado, tomato and green onion. Mix. Serve and add toppings such as salsa, sour cream and lime juice to taste.

Naan Bread Pizza

-Naan Bread -Olive Oil -Cheese -Pizza Sauce -Toppings

Pre-heat oven to 400° F. (toster oven or normal oven) Take Naan and spread olive oil lightly on both sides. Bake for 30-45 secs. Take out Naan, add sauce, cheese and your favourite toppings. Bake till cheese is lightly golden brown or as cooked as you like. Remove from oven and let cool for 1-2 mins then cut it and enjoy.

Sriracha Noodles

1brick 1 tbsp 1 tbsp 1 cup 1/2 cup 2 tbsp -Noodles -Sesame Oil -Sesame Seeds -Raw Spinach -Broccoli -Sriracha

Boil and set aside noodles, if you used noodles from a ramen pack save the seasoning for another time! Sauté sesame seeds in sesame oil till lightly toasted. Add in spinach and broccoli, once spinach has cooked down add in the noodles and sriracha. Add more sriracha if you want the noodles more saucy and spicy. NOTE Sesame seeds are optional.

Bulked Up Greek Salad

Take the olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, vinegar and oregano and shake in jar until blended. Season to taste with salt and black pepper. Combine the chopped tomatoes, cucumber, onion, bell pepper, olives and drained, rinsed chickpeas in a bowl. Toss with dressing. Sprinkle cheese over and serve. NOTE Best after refrigerated for a few hours or overnight. Chickpeas add protein and make it more filling. Add spinach or other greens for extra nutrition. Add Italian or Greek Seasoning in dressing for extra flavour or if you don't have oregano
